What does try do

TRY is a nonprofit startup tackling widespread violence in communities. We provide high school students with a multidimensional curriculum, giving youth the tools and skills to reduce violence and crime in their own neighborhoods.,

Where we will start

We will start in Nazareth, a densely populated area with a lot of violence and crime. We believe it is a great place for TRY to start developing a youth club. Through this, the youth of Nazareth can have a safe space for them to attend and interact with many other teens, as well as experience a unique multidimensional curriculum. At the beginning of our startup, we will open a youth club in Nazareth hosting 30 high school students who are social leaders in the community and will experience different fun, educational, and anti-violence centered activities during their time at the club. ,
