What does try do

TRY is a nonprofit startup tackling widespread violence in communities. We provide high school students with a multidimensional curriculum, giving youth the tools and skills to reduce violence and crime in their own neighborhoods.

Where we will start

We will start in Nazareth, a densely populated area with a lot of violence and crime. We believe it is a great place for TRY to start developing a youth club. Through this, the youth of Nazareth can have a safe space for them to attend and interact with many other teens.

Why Us?

Teens for teens

We understand youth needs and how to create the perfect experience for them, simply because we are youth!!

Develop professional skills

We introduce job professionals and present a whole employment based curriculum, which includes strong pre-professional training for the youth.

Vibrant and dynamic programs

We offer different curriculums which include many fun activities, team-building exercises, and an anti-violence curriculum.
